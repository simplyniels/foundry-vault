British neuroscientist and sleep researcher; Professor of Neuroscience and Psychology at UC Berkeley. Director of the Center for Human Sleep Science. His book Why We Sleep (Das Große Buch Vom Schlaf in German) is the defining popular science account of sleep biology and the consequences of sleep deprivation.

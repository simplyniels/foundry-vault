## Prompts

Essay-shaped prompts from concept articles. These are outputs aimed at your writing — "you could write this now because your notes are 80% of the way there."

### From [[Two-layer knowledge systems]]
- *Where the authorship line should sit.* The tension between LLM-owns-the-wiki and human-curation-as-taste.
- *Two-layer as a writing discipline.* Human-curated source + AI-derived output applied to writing.

### From [[Agentic AI as Workforce]]
- *The CLAUDE.md question.* What are the three rules your current AI sessions keep forgetting? Write them, encode them, and track how the compounding works over six months.
- *Write the briefing doc.* Brief a new AI agent on your workflow the way you'd brief a junior hire on day one — what do they need to know to not make expensive mistakes?

### From [[AI as Conversation Partner Not Oracle]]
- *Rewrite your prompts as conversation openers.* Take your three most-used prompts and redesign them as the first message in a dialogue, not a command.

### From [[PARA as Destination-First Organisation]]
- *Apply the kitchen test.* Map your current filing structure and mark which items are organised by origin vs. destination. Redesign it destination-first.

### From [[Attention as the Scarce Resource]]
- *The honest attention budget.* What is currently getting your best attention that shouldn't be? Write the real version, not the aspirational one.

### From [[Foundation Health Triad]]
- *Audit the triad.* Sleep quality, diet pattern, breathing habits — which one is your biggest liability? Start there.

### From [[Customer-First Entrepreneurship]]
- *The presale weekend test.* Pick one idea you've been sitting on. Design the minimum presale test you could run this weekend.

### From [[Positive Performance Psychology]]
- *The diagnostic test.* Pick one area where your productivity tactics are failing. Is the problem the tactic, or is the emotional precondition not met?

### From [[Model Cost-Quality Optimisation in AI Agents]]
- *Design your router.* If you had to decide when to call Opus vs. Sonnet vs. Haiku, what signals would it use? Task type? Confidence? Consequence of error?

### From [[AI Embedded in Personal Productivity Tools]]
- *Map your ideal embedded AI workflow.* Reading → thinking → writing → reviewing. Where would AI enhancement be invisible and where would it be intrusive?

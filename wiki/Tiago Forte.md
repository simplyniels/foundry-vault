Writer, educator, and creator of the Building a Second Brain (BASB) framework. Forte developed the CODE method (Capture, Organise, Distill, Express) and the PARA organisational system (Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives) as a systematic approach to personal knowledge management. His work is the dominant influence on the second-brain and PKM space.

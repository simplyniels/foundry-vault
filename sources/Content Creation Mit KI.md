**Summary**

Andreas Berens and Carsten Bolk's practical guide to AI-assisted content creation, focused on GPT-4 capabilities. The book covers the shift to multimodal AI (text + images + code) and positions GPT-4 as a fundamental change in what's possible for content creators — from generating copy to programming websites from a sketch, reviewing contracts, and creating tax declarations.

**Key points**
- GPT stands for Generative Pre-trained Transformer
- GPT-4 can process up to 25,000 words of input at once (at launch)
- GPT-4 is multimodal: reads and interprets images, not just text
- Practical capabilities: website from a pencil sketch, contract review for legal errors, tax filing assistance, video game programming on command
- Multimodality = multiple sensory modalities (text, image) processed and integrated in output

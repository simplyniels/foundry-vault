**Summary**

Birgit Haustedt's curated insider guide to Venice's favourite places. Rather than a standard tourist guide, it assembles personal recommendations — specific cafés, the best coffee in the city, the atmospheric experience of night-time water travel. A mood guide as much as an itinerary.

**Key points**
- Perfect Venice morning: start at Caffè del Doge (hidden; claimed best coffee in the city), then Rialto fish market
- Night gondola travel: lit windows revealing chandeliers, gilded wooden ceilings, ceiling frescos — spectacle at a human scale
- Caffè del Doge's house blend: extra mild roast created in the 1950s by Cavalier Ermenegildo Rizzardini

**Claude's notes**

Sparse highlights but evocative — Haustedt writes about Venice as atmosphere rather than catalogue. The Caffè del Doge recommendation is specific and testable.

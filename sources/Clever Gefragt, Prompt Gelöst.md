**Summary**

A German practical guide to prompt engineering for everyday tasks, structured around 77 question templates covering health, fitness, nutrition, sleep, finance, career, and content creation. The core framework is a four-block prompt structure: Role + Task + Context + Output. The book's thesis is that AI delivers dramatically better results when given explicit framing — the quality of answers is a direct function of the quality of questions.

**Key points**
- Four prompt blocks: Role (who should the AI be?), Task (what specifically?), Context (your situation and details), Output (what format and depth?)
- Treat AI as a conversation, not a one-shot query — iterative dialogue ("Pingpong") is where best results emerge
- Role assignment shapes the AI's perspective and expertise level before it responds
- Context is the most commonly missing block: AI can't know what you know; fill in the gap explicitly
- The "context question prompt": ask the AI what it needs to know before it answers, to offload the burden of completeness
- Content blocks: structured prompt templates for blog posts, social media, recipe planning, evening routines, exercise plans, job search, personal branding
- For humaniSing AI text: prompt it to revise its own output with a fresh natural-language persona

**Summary**

Rainer Hattenhauer's guide to ChatGPT and generative AI tools for general audiences. The book covers model capabilities, prompt-writing for image generation, AI-based time tracking tools, and multimodal functionality (GPT-4's ability to process images, including building websites from hand-drawn sketches).

**Key points**
- GPT-4 is multimodal — trained on image data and can process visually fed inputs
- The GPT-4 website-from-sketch capability is a landmark example of multimodal potential
- For image-generating AI (Dall-E, Midjourney etc.), English-language prompts still produce better results than German
- Timely (timelyapp.com): AI-based time-tracking tool
- The author's personal AI breakthrough: Christmas 2023 in Doucy, after an earlier introduction to ChatGPT Plus by a contact in Malmö in December

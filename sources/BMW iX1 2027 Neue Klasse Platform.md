**Summary**
The 2027 BMW iX1 will adopt the Neue Klasse electric platform introduced by the iX3, bringing 20% higher energy density (round vs. prismatic cells), 800V architecture supporting up to 400 kW charging. The iX3 can add 372 km of range in 10 minutes. The iX1's design will closely follow the larger iX3. Performance expected to be similar to the current iX1.

**Key points**
- 2027 iX1 on Neue Klasse platform — first confirmed for small SUV segment
- New round-cell batteries: 20% higher energy density vs. current prismatic cells
- 800V architecture: up to 400 kW charge speed (10 min = 372 km for iX3)
- Design closely follows iX3 — family appearance standardising across electric range

**Claude's notes**
The Neue Klasse battery spec (round cells, 800V, 400 kW) represents BMW's full leap to next-generation EV architecture. 400 kW charging pushes range anxiety near zero for urban use cases. The iX1 getting this platform in 2027 suggests the technology will be broadly deployed across BMW's lineup, not restricted to halo models.

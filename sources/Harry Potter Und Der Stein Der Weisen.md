**Summary**

J.K. Rowling's first Harry Potter novel (German translation by Klaus Fritz). Harry Potter discovers he is a wizard and enters Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he confronts Voldemort's first attempt to return to power. A foundational work of contemporary fantasy fiction.

**Key points**
- "Ich würde Hagrid mein Leben anvertrauen" — Dumbledore's expression of absolute trust
- "Es ist nicht gut, wenn wir nur unseren Träumen nachhängen und vergessen zu leben" — Dumbledore on the danger of living only in fantasy
- "Das Problem ist, die Menschen haben den Hang, genau das zu wählen, was am schlechtesten für sie ist" — human tendency toward self-defeating choices
- Dudley's manipulative behaviour (tantrums, violence) as an early portrait of overindulged character

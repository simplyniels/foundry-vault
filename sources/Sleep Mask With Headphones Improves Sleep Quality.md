**Summary**
A product article on a sleep mask with integrated flat headphones (INF brand), covering the research backing both components. A 2022 Cochrane meta-analysis confirms music shortens sleep onset and improves sleep duration. Cardiff University research shows nighttime light disrupts circadian rhythm; masks reduce this with measurable benefits for next-day concentration and attention. The flat design accommodates side-sleepers.

**Key points**
- Music for sleep: 2022 Cochrane summary — shortens sleep onset, improves sleep duration, works as acoustic "winding down"
- Light blocking for sleep: Cardiff University — nighttime light disrupts day-night cycle; masks improve next-day concentration and attention
- Combined effect: darkness + acoustic isolation = optimal physiological conditions for sleep onset and maintenance
- Flat-headphone design solves side-sleeper comfort problem

**Summary**

Tim Ferriss's compendium of tactics, habits, and advice collected from hundreds of interviews with world-class performers — athletes, entrepreneurs, scientists, artists. The book distils patterns from peak performers across domains into a portable reference of frameworks, morning routines, and decision-making heuristics.

**Key points**
- Established large corporations fail to adapt when management is incentivised by quarterly/annual targets rather than long-term change
- A clear vision of where you want to go makes everything simpler — direction replaces decision paralysis

**Summary**
Apple App Store editorial feature covering three AI apps: ChatGPT as an all-rounder (writing, coding, document analysis, custom GPTs), Perplexity for research with cited sources (essays, journalism, market research), and Claude for long structured texts and complex documents (philosophy, science, sensitive topics). Claude is highlighted for integrations with Notion, Google Drive, and Gmail.

**Key points**

- Perplexity: research and fact-checking with source citations — "a reassuring level of transparency"
- Claude: long structured texts, complex documents, philosophical/scientific depth; Notion/Google Drive/Gmail integrations

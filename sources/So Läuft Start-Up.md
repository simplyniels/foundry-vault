**Summary**

Florian Gschwandtner's memoir of building Runtastic, the fitness app company he co-founded in Austria and sold to Adidas. The book covers team culture, motivation, leadership philosophy, and the startup experience from founder's perspective. Written as motivational case study for aspiring entrepreneurs in German-speaking markets.

**Key points**
- Motivation for top performance: clear objective + preparation + external sources of inspiration
- Runtastic's vision: every individual pursuing a more conscious and active lifestyle, leading to higher life expectancy and fulfilment
- TEAM — Together Everyone Achieves More
- "Playing to win" versus "playing not to lose" — a fundamental mindset divide
- Thursday thank-you notes as a practice for team gratitude culture
- "Start with the why" (Simon Sinek reference) — purpose-driven leadership
- Efficiency in personal habits matters: even breakfast choices are optimised

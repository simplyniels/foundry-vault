**Summary**
Amazon rolled out AI note-taking features to 2022 and 2024 Kindle Scribe models in the US, UK, Canada, and Australia. The update also adds a side panel for writing notes while reading ebooks. This makes the Kindle Scribe more competitive as an e-paper reading and note-taking tablet.

**Key points**
- AI note-taking features now on Kindle Scribe 2022 and 2024 models
- Rollout: US, UK, Canada, Australia
- New side panel: write notes alongside ebook text
- Positions Kindle Scribe better against competing e-paper tablets
